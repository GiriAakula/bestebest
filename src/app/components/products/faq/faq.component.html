I rely on customer ratings on the E-com sites. Is that wrong?

Most customers rely on customer ratings to buy. There is nothing wrong with it. However, customer ratings may not reflect reality and may not be helpful at all times. Example, a product is overlooked due to no ratings or fewer ratings. Displaying global customer ratings from a different country to buy locally can be misled into sub-optimal choices. The significant finding is what is called "choice bias". Once the customer buys a product unless the product is shoddy, they wouldn't rate it adversely because they chose to buy it.
